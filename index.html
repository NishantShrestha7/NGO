    • WHO WE ARE

    NGO Federation of Nepal (NFN) is an umbrella organization or national platform of 6517 NGOs working in various fields of social welfare and development in Nepal. Established in 1991 for the promotion and protection of social justice, human rights, civic space and pro-poor development, NFN has evolved as a leading organization working for the entire NGO movement in Nepal.

    NEPAL SDGS FORUM
